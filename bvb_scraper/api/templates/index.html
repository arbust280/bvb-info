COLUMN LEGEND

SYM
The instrument's ticker symbol on the Bucharest Stock Exchange — the short code used in every trade, quote and API call (e.g. TLV for Banca Transilvania). Click it to open the company page.
COMPANY
The issuer's registered name. Some rows are investment funds or ETFs rather than companies.
PRICE
The last price at which the instrument traded during this session, in Romanian lei (RON) per share/unit.
VAR%
Percentage change of the last price versus the previous session's reference (closing) price. Green means it closed up, red down, grey unchanged.
OPEN
The price of the first trade of the session.
HIGH
The highest trade price reached during the session.
LOW
The lowest trade price reached during the session.
VOLUME
How many shares/units changed hands over the whole session, across all trades.
VALUE RON
The total value traded during the session, in lei — the sum of price × shares over every individual trade. The table is sorted by this column, so the most actively traded instruments appear first.
MKT
The BVB market segment where the instrument trades: REGS is the main regulated market; XRS1 is the SMT/AeRO alternative market for smaller issuers, which typically trades less frequently.
—
An em-dash means the exchange published no figure — common for fund units and for instruments with no trades in the session.
